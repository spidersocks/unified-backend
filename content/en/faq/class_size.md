# Class Size — Group Size FAQ

Q: How many students are there in each class?  
A: Most of our group classes have 4–6 students per group. Some older or advanced classes may have up to 8 students.

Q: Do you offer one-on-one (private) lessons?  
A: Yes, private lessons can be arranged if requested.

Q: Are classes always small groups?  
A: Yes. We believe small groups provide more attention for each child and a better learning experience.

Q: What's the maximum class size?  
A: Group classes are normally capped at 4–6 students; some upper primary groups may have up to 8.
