# Tuition FAQ

## General

Q: What is the tuition? / How much is the fee? / What are your class fees?  
A: We offer different courses with different fee schedules. Could you tell us which course you would like to know the tuition for? (E.g., English, Chinese, Mandarin, Playgroup, Math, Phonics, etc.)

---

## English Courses

Q: How much is your English class? / English Language Arts tuition?  
A: English group class tuition: HK$4,500 for 8 sessions (45 minutes/session); HK$7,500–$8,500 for 10 sessions (1 hour/session), depending on instructor.

Q: What is the fee for phonics classes?  
A: Phonics class: HK$5,200 for 8×45min; HK$7,500–$8,500 for 10×1h.

---

## Chinese Courses

Q: What is the tuition for your Chinese class? / Chinese Language Arts fee?  
A: Chinese group class: HK$3,200 for 8 sessions (45 minutes/session); HK$4,500 for 10 sessions (1 hour/session).

Q: Chinese writing class fee?  
A: Same as Chinese group class: HK$3,200 per 8×45m; HK$4,500 per 10×1h.
