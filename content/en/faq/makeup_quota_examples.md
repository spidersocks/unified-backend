# Make-up Quota Examples

Q: Thanks, so if we take holiday, we will need to take it out of our quota (ie 2 holidays per 10 class)?  
A: Every student is entitled to 2 free make-up lessons for each 8 paid sessions OR 3 free make-up lessons for each 10 paid sessions.
