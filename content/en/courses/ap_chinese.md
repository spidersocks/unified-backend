# AP Chinese — Course Overview

Who is this for?
- Secondary students preparing for AP Chinese Language and Culture (College Board).

What we cover
- Reading: text types, fast comprehension, question types and distractors.
- Listening: note‑taking, gist/detail distinction, accent familiarity (standard Putonghua).
- Speaking: presentational and interpersonal tasks; pronunciation clarity and fluency.
- Writing: email reply and presentational writing; structure, register and accuracy.
- Culture: topics required by the AP syllabus woven through tasks.

How we teach
- Placement first, then a plan that balances skill building with exam‑style practice.
- Regular feedback and timed drills; progress tracked against AP scoring criteria.

Logistics
- Typical lesson length: 60 minutes weekly (frequency can be adjusted).
- Delivery: In‑person at our centre; Zoom can be arranged when appropriate.
